If any orders contained Fresh Fish and delivery slot fall on Sunday, delivery will be subject to availability of Fresh Fish and Meat from the central market and may be postponed to next day. Current Lead Time for Order execution is 4 to 6 hours Current Delivery Offset time is 5 hours (Minimum Time between the ordering and next delivery slot availability)
